Brief Biography 

Mr. Zi Liu obtained his B.S. degree in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering from Anhui Polytechnic University in 2012. Subsequently, he pursued a master's degree in Mechanical and Electronic Engineering at Jingdezhen Ceramic University from 2012 to 2016, where he contributed to the Jingdezhen Key Laboratory of Biological Big Data under Professor Xuan Xiao's leadership. As a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanjing University of Science and Technology, he was actively involved in the Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group, led by Professor Dong-Jun Yu. Between 2017 and 2019, Mr. Liu enhanced his research expertise as a visiting student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, under the auspices of the China Scholarship Council and Professor Yang Zhang's guidance. Currently, he serves as a faculty member at Jingdezhen Ceramic University's School of Information Engineering and continues his association with the Jingdezhen Key Laboratory of Biological Big Data, now under Professor Wang-Ren Qiu. His research interests span bioinformatics, pattern recognition, machine learning, and data mining.

He is interested in the following topics

  • Protein-Protein Interation Prediction
